About me

Norbert Szczepanik

A foot care specialist with heart – and more than a decade of experience as a caregiver in a nursing home.

My path to foot care

My professional roots are in care work: since 2012 I have been working as a caregiver in a nursing home, supporting people who need help in their everyday lives – with attentiveness, patience and respect. During this time I have seen every day how much well-groomed feet matter for mobility, safety and quality of life: those who are steady on their feet stay independent.

In 2024 I put this experience on a professional footing and completed my training as a foot care specialist at the Fußpflegeschule FAY foot care school. From expert nail and cuticle care and gentle callus removal to the disinfection and hygienic preparation of my instruments, I work to the standards I am used to from care work: careful, structured and with no compromises on hygiene.

As a mobile foot care specialist, I come to wherever you feel most comfortable: your own home. Especially for senior citizens, for people with limited mobility or with a packed schedule, a home visit makes life noticeably easier – no travelling, no waiting room, familiar surroundings. I bring all the professional equipment with me and take the time your feet need.

My promise: From your first enquiry to the finished care, you should feel in good hands – professionally and personally.
